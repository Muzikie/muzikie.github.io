While music streaming platform such as Apple Music and spotify have made music significantly more accessible, they have done so by putting a dent in the income of artists.

How it is now

Payment

Miniscule, delayed, and unfair payment of streaming giants to artists has caused frustration in the music community and killed countless talents.

Transparency

Lack of a reliable and transparent approach for calculating the number of streams, downloads, and broadcasts of songs.

Fundraising

Indie artists struggle to raise capital to bring their ideas to life.

Ownership

Lack of true ownership of digital assets has left artists with no control over the use & distribution of their artwork.

I Need a Dollar

The most streamed single of 2013 on Spotify earned its authors $4,000 each.


“ The System just ain't working
   There must be some mistake
   When the only way i could get paid

Aloe Blacc is not the only artists who has taken their music off Spotify. Taylor Swift has also removed her music from the platform. The reason is simple: Spotify pays artists a fraction of a penny per stream. The average artist earns less than $1,000 per year from Spotify.

Muzikie solutions

Muzikie offers an auditable, democratic and modern solution to these issues. Benefiting from the blockchain technology Muzikie is a platform for young and promising artists to grow and shine to the fullest of their potential.

Fairness

Muzikie pays a whopping 80% of the total system profit to the artists. There are additional direct sale approaches to provide artists with flexible income channels.
Also, audience in the ad-supported plan receive a 10% share of ads profit!

Purchase
  • Artist may offer different licenses for downloading their music and videos and using them in other projects

Transparency

Build on Lisk blockchain, streaming count, loyalty right distributions, system income and artists’ share are transparently available to everyone.

Shared loyalties:
  • Users may split shares of their loyalty rights right inside the app

Sponsorship

Musicians can pitch the idea of their next album to fans, and raise funds. Fans can support their trusted artists in exchange for concert tickets, merchandise, or even a share in the future income.

Play your role in music
  • Support your favorite up and coming artists

Ownership

Using Muzikie, artists now own their songs, albums, and playlists with their entire histories. They can also freely sell or distribute loyalty rights of their songs right in the application.

Blockchain and NFTs:
