Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try for free?

Absolutely! Muzikie gives everyone a free subscription for 2 month, in exchange for 2 simple social media shares!

What payment methods do you accept?

Payments in Muzikie are all based on MZK tokens. But don't worry, they're easy to buy!

Who else can make money?

If you are a music curator, a DJ, or you just have great playlists, you can monetize them directly. Also, users who adopt to the ad-driven plan, receive a 10% share of ads profit.

How are artists paid?

Artists receive an 80% share of the total profit, available to claim at the end of every month, even if it's a penny!
